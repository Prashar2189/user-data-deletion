User Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 11 August 2025

If you have an account on Hola salesN and want your data removed, follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to raghav.prashar@appsimity.com with the subject: Delete My Data.
  2. In the email, include the full name and the email address you used to register.
  3. We will confirm receipt and delete your data from our systems within 7 business days.
  4. After deletion, we will send you a confirmation email.

If you logged in via Facebook

You can remove Hola salesN's access from your Facebook account as follows:

  • Go to Facebook → Settings & privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites.
  • Under "Active", find Hola salesN and click Remove.
  • Removing the app from Facebook revokes further access to your Facebook data but does not by itself delete data already stored by Hola salesN — to remove stored data, send the email above.

Questions

If you have questions about data deletion or your data rights, contact us at raghav.prashar@appsimity.com.
